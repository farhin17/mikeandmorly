Welcome to Mike and Morlly Architects, where creativity, innovation, and expertise converge to shape extraordinary spaces. Our team is led by two visionary architects, Mike and Morlly, who bring a wealth of experience and a shared passion for delivering exceptional design solutions.

Together, Mike and Morlly foster a collaborative and client-centric approach to architecture. They believe that the most successful designs are born from a deep understanding of their clients' aspirations, needs, and unique project requirements. Their collaborative process encourages open communication, active involvement, and a shared vision between the architects, clients, and project stakeholders.

At Mike and Morlly Architects, we strive to deliver design solutions that not only exceed expectations but also create spaces that inspire, engage, and enrich lives. By leveraging our extensive expertise and staying at the forefront of industry trends, we ensure that our designs are at the cutting edge of architectural innovation.

Our team

Michael MurdockDirector

With over a decade of experience in the architectural industry, Mike has established himself as a respected authority in the field. Known for his keen eye for detail and unwavering commitment to excellence, Mike's designs reflect a deep understanding of aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability.

Throughout his career, Mike has successfully executed a diverse range of projects, including luxury residential developments, commercial spaces, and cultural institutions. His ability to seamlessly blend form and function has earned him recognition for creating spaces that are not only visually captivating but also highly practical and user-centric.

  • Architect NSW ARB 11538
  • Architect VIC ARB 19753
  • Registered Design Practitioner Fair Trading DEP0003411

Morlly KimDirector / Principal

Morlly is a highly accomplished architect with a flair for pushing boundaries and embracing innovation. Her passion for architecture extends beyond conventional design principles, as she constantly seeks to challenge norms and explore new possibilities. Morlly's work is characterised by bold, forward-thinking concepts that redefine spaces and enhance the human experience. With an extensive portfolio encompassing landmark projects in urban planning, hospitality, and public spaces, Morlly has made a lasting impact on the architectural landscape. Her ability to envision and execute designs that seamlessly blend creativity, functionality, and sustainability has garnered acclaim from both clients and industry peers alike.

  • Architect VIC ARB 19753
